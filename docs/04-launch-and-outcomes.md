04 · Launch & Outcomes

Launch

Following design, development, testing, and stakeholder review, the website was deployed as the primary online presence for Bins & Deals.

The website became the central destination for customers seeking information about the business, pricing, inventory categories, store hours, and location.

⸻

Business Impact

Following launch, the owner reported positive feedback regarding both the design and usability of the website.

The website helped consolidate important business information into a single destination that customers could access quickly from search engines, social media, and direct referrals.

⸻

Customer Experience Improvements

The website improved customer access to:

* Pricing information
* Product categories
* Store location
* Contact information
* Store hours
* Facebook updates
* Marketplace listings

This reduced friction for first-time visitors and made information easier to discover.

⸻

Owner Feedback

The business owner expressed satisfaction with the final result and indicated that the website helped strengthen the store’s online presence.

The project successfully translated business requirements into a customer-friendly digital experience while maintaining flexibility for future growth.

⸻

Project Outcomes

Business Outcomes

* Professional online presence established
* Improved customer communication
* Better visibility for local customers
* Stronger digital credibility

User Outcomes

* Easier access to store information
* Improved understanding of pricing
* Faster access to contact and location details
* Better mobile experience

Technical Outcomes

* Responsive website
* Production deployment
* SEO implementation
* Maintainable React codebase
* Scalable architecture for future updates

⸻

Reflection

One of the most valuable lessons from this project was the importance of understanding real customer behavior before making design decisions.

By combining stakeholder input with direct customer conversations, the final experience addressed actual user needs rather than assumptions.

The project reinforced the value of research-driven design, clear communication, and iterative problem solving in real business environments.
