01 · Business Context

Project Overview

Bins & Deals is a local liquidation retail store located in Liberty, Missouri. The business purchases liquidation, overstock, returned, and surplus inventory and resells products to customers at heavily discounted prices.

The store operates a treasure-hunt shopping model where inventory changes frequently and customers visit regularly to discover new deals across categories such as electronics, home goods, tools, clothing, toys, and household products.

The objective of this project was to create a professional online presence that clearly communicates the business model, improves customer understanding, and makes important store information easy to access.

⸻

Business Challenge

Prior to this project, the business relied primarily on in-store traffic, word-of-mouth referrals, Facebook activity, and local awareness.

Several challenges were identified during discussions with the owner:

* Many first-time customers did not understand how the pricing system worked.
* Store information was spread across different channels.
* Customers frequently called or visited social media pages to ask about store hours, location, and available product categories.
* The business lacked a dedicated website that could serve as a central source of information.
* There was an opportunity to improve trust and credibility through a professional digital presence.

⸻

Project Goals

The website was designed to achieve several business objectives:

* Clearly explain the store concept.
* Make pricing easy to understand.
* Showcase product categories available in-store.
* Improve visibility for local customers.
* Increase confidence and trust among first-time visitors.
* Provide quick access to store hours, location, and contact information.
* Support social media marketing efforts.
* Create a foundation for future growth and online discoverability.
